Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information CountDracula (the "App"), operated by Hayes McNeill, collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding it. The App is designed to work fully without an account — signing in is optional and only needed to sync your Blood Drops balance across devices or make a purchase.

2. Information We Collect

DataWhenWhy
Email addressOnly if you create an accountSign-in identity, password reset
Gameplay stats & Blood Drops balanceStored locally always; synced to our database only if signed inSave your progress and currency balance across sessions/devices
Purchase recordsOnly when you buy Blood VialsPrevent duplicate credit, provide support

We never collect or store your full payment card number — that's handled entirely by Stripe (see Section 4).

3. How We Use Information

Information is used only to operate the App: authenticating your account, syncing your Blood Drops balance, crediting purchases, and providing support if you contact us. We do not sell your data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App relies on a small number of third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — account sign-in and data storage.
  • Stripe — payment processing for Blood Vial purchases.
  • Netlify — website and backend hosting.

These providers may process technical data (such as IP address) as part of standard hosting/security operations, independent of anything we specifically request.

5. Data Retention

Local gameplay data stays on your device until you clear it. Account data (email, synced balance) is retained until you request deletion or your account is otherwise removed.

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing councilcomputing@gmail.com. You can also stop using an account entirely and continue using the App as a guest — all local gameplay data will remain on your device regardless.

7. Children's Privacy

CountDracula is intended for users 18 years of age and older, per our Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

8. Security

We use industry-standard providers (Firebase, Stripe) that maintain their own security practices for the data they process. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to councilcomputing@gmail.com.
