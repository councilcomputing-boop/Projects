Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 14, 2026

Short version: Your prayer data stays on your device or your own server. We don't sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. Ever.

1. Who We Are

Prayer Profiles is a personal prayer-tracking application developed by Council Computing. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at councilcomputing@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

When you use Prayer Profiles, the following information is stored:

  • Account information: your chosen username and a bcrypt-hashed (one-way encrypted) version of your password. Your plain-text password is never stored.
  • Prayer profiles: names, relationship labels, phone numbers, email addresses, birthdays, and personal notes you enter for each person you pray for.
  • Prayer notes: the text of prayer requests you log, and — when you mark one answered — the date/time it was answered and any note you write about how it was answered.
  • Bible verses: scripture references and verse text you attach to profiles.
  • Profile photos: images you upload are stored as files on the server.
  • Prayer activity: the date and time you last marked each person as "Prayed Today", and a per-day log of the days you prayed (used to show your streak and daily progress).
  • Reminders: if you create reminders, we store the reminder schedule (person or group, days, and time), your timezone, and — if you enable notifications — the push subscription address your browser generates for this device.
  • Aggregate usage counters: anonymous daily totals across all users — number of sign-ins, prayer requests added, prayers marked answered, verses added, and "prayed today" marks. These counters contain no usernames and no prayer, note, or verse content, and cannot be traced back to an individual user.

We do not collect location data, advertising identifiers, or any content beyond what you explicitly enter. The only usage measurement is the anonymous aggregate counters described above.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in a JSON file and an uploads folder on the server or device running Prayer Profiles:

  • Desktop (Electron) app: data is stored locally on your own computer in your OS user-data directory. It never leaves your machine.
  • Self-hosted web deployment: data is stored on the server you control (e.g., Fly.io, your own VPS). You are responsible for that server's security and backups.

4. iCloud Contact Sync

Prayer Profiles includes an optional feature to import contacts from iCloud. When you use this feature:

  • Your Apple ID and app-specific password are transmitted to the Prayer Profiles server over HTTPS.
  • These credentials are used only to make a one-time CardDAV request to Apple's servers and are never stored, logged, or retained after the request completes.
  • We strongly recommend using an app-specific password (not your primary Apple ID password) for this feature.

5. Usage Analytics (Aggregated & Anonymized)

To understand whether the app is healthy and being used, the server keeps daily aggregate counters: total sign-ins, prayer requests added, prayers marked answered, verses added, and "prayed today" marks across all users combined.

  • Counters are numbers only — no usernames, user IDs, prayer text, names, or verse content are ever recorded in them.
  • They cannot be used to reconstruct who prayed, for whom, or about what.
  • They are visible only to the operator through a key-protected report page, and are never shared or sold.

Your prayer content is never used in any report.

6. Reminder Notifications

If you turn on reminder notifications:

  • Your browser creates a push subscription (a delivery address operated by your browser vendor — e.g., Apple or Google) which we store to deliver your reminders.
  • Notification messages contain only the person's name or group name you chose to be reminded about — they are sent through your browser vendor's push service to reach your device.
  • Your timezone is stored so reminders arrive at the local time you picked.
  • You can turn notifications off at any time in your device or browser settings; dead subscriptions are automatically removed.

7. Third-Party Services

Prayer Profiles connects to the following external services:

  • Google Fonts — The app loads fonts from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log your IP address when serving these fonts. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Browser push services — Only if you enable reminder notifications (see Section 6), reminder messages are delivered through your browser vendor's push infrastructure (e.g., Apple Push or Google FCM).
  • Apple iCloud CardDAV — Only when you explicitly use the iCloud sync feature, as described in Section 4.

Verse text is entered or pasted by you directly — the app no longer sends verse references to any external Bible service. We have no advertising networks, no third-party analytics services, and no social login providers. Our hosting provider (Fly.io) may keep standard infrastructure logs (such as request IP addresses) as part of operating the platform.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your prayer data is yours alone.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained until you delete it. You can delete individual profiles, prayer notes, and verses from within the app. To permanently delete all your data, delete your account data file from the server or contact us.

10. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (work factor 12) and are never stored in plain text.
  • Sessions use signed JSON Web Tokens (JWT) that expire after 30 days.
  • The web version enforces HTTPS.
  • API endpoints are rate-limited to reduce brute-force risk.
  • Security headers (CSP, X-Frame-Options, etc.) are applied to all responses.

No system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee the security of data you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk.

11. Children's Privacy

Prayer Profiles is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

13. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your data, contact us at councilcomputing@gmail.com.
